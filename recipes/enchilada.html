Chicken Enchiladas

What's in it?

Gooey, melty mozzarella and Jack cheese, taco-seasoned shredded chicken, sour cream and corn tortillas, all slathered in mildly tangy green enchilada sauce and baked until your house smells like your favorite Mexican restaurant (in the very best way).

Red enchilada sauce may also be called salsa roja, salsa roja para enchiladas or mole rojo. It is a sauce made out of ancho/ pasilla/New Mexico and/or Cascabel chiles, onion, garlic, tomatoes, broth, cumin, oregano, etc. all blended together then simmered until thickened.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken
  • Monterey Jack Cheese
  • Tortilla
  • Green enchilada sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. Shred the chicken
  3. Warm the tortillas in a microwave for 1 minute
  4. Assemble the tortilla evenly with chicken
  5. pour the enchilada sauce on them alon with the cheese
